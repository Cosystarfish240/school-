Welcome

We always dreamt things that never were and said ‘why not’. We wanted to make a difference in the lives of children. We challenge stereotypical beliefs about intelligence, ability and academic excellence; for we believe all students are capable of total effort that would lead to good outcomes. Our aspiration is to foster an atmosphere where all students feel they are valued and their individuality is recognized and nurtured thereby learn in a non-threatening, non-competitive and joyful atmosphere. It was our dream to build a sense of community in the classroom and let the students know it is their class.
Vision
“To equip students with the 21st century skills in a safe learning environment by providing a balanced, varied school curriculum designed to meet the academic, cultural and social needs of learners from diverse backgrounds who will become responsible citizens, lifelong learners, and future leaders.”
